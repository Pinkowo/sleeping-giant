About Hastings

Hastings, Hawke's Bay

Hastings is a city in the Hawke's Bay region on the east coast of the North Island of New Zealand. It is the largest inland city in Hawke's Bay and part of the wider Hastings District. The surrounding plains are known for orchards, vineyards, and a strong food and wine culture.
