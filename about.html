About Sleeping Giant Backpackers

The Sleeping Giant Backpackers located in Hastings NZ is a working hostel set in a comfortable character villa with large rooms, high ceilings and a homely feel. We are located close to many work places like orchards, vineyards, pack houses, and factories and we supply many good employers.

At a glance

  • Character villa with large rooms
  • Close to orchards and pack houses
  • Homely, long-stay friendly

Hastings is a great working stop and we are close to Hastings Pak 'N Save supermarket, the hospital, post office, banks, chemist, sports fields and everything you need for your working holiday.
