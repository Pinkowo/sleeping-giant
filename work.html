Work Opportunities

At Sleeping Giant Backpackers we help you get work. There is work all year round in Hastings and we can help you get a job.

High season

  • October to May
  • Pruning, thinning, harvest
  • Orchards and pack houses

The high season is October - May. The 3 main periods are:

  • May-October (Pruning): Cutting and tying the apple and kiwi fruit branches. Pack house work as well.
  • October-December (Thinning): Picking off the extra fruit.
  • December-May (Harvest): Picking apples, pears, kiwi, raspberries, grapes.

Sleeping Giant Backpackers has an arrangement with many of the employers at local orchards, vineyards, and pack houses.
